What Is Zunder Zifflin?

Zunder Zifflin is one of a well known paper company based in Alexandra, Virginia and was formed in November 2002 by Harry Harris and James Jameson. For over a decades, Harry Harris as a Founder of this company has a unique philosophy. "Zunder Zifflin is dedicated to providing enviromentally preferable packaging and supplies. Through our Green & Clean Program, Zunder Zifflin has access to the most innovative and sustainable products in the marketplace."
